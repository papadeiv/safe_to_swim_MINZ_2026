# Safe to swim
## A preliminary study of the E.coli dynamics in Aotearoa New Zealand's rivers
This is a group project in response to a proposed challenge at the [Mathematics in Industry NZ](https://minz.org.nz/) conference held at the Auckland University of Technology between the 22nd and the 26th of June 2026.
